Welcome to the team! This guide covers everything you need to get up to speed — tools, codebase, process, and culture.

  

Note: Your manager will be Chris Baron however, we don’t believe in strict hierarchy and function as a flat organization. 

  

About Heylo

Heylo provides smart home hardware and software to provider agencies that support adults with intellectual and developmental disabilities (I/DD). Our mission is to give caregivers the tools they need to deliver better, safer support for the residents they serve.

|Ruthless prioritization|We say no to what doesn’t improve Heylo for our customers. Protect team capacity and stay aligned with the mission.|
|Reliable product|While issues this early are expected, how quick we respond and resolve issues are critical. Every bug and inconsistent experience is a crack in customer trust.|
|Relentless growth|Build, ship, and learn fast.|

# 2. Tooling & Systems

-  Linear: engineering tickets, sprint planning 
    
-  Notion: team wiki, meeting notes, planning docs, PRDs
    
- Github
    
- AWS
    
- Slack: team communication
    
- Intercom: customer support, live chat, escalation tracking (still setting up)
    

  
  

# 3. How We Work

- Linear cycles & projects - two week sprints.
    
- Weekly Stand-up on mondays 
    
- L10 meetings weekly — bring blockers and wins
    
- Any customer facing feature will require two reviewers 
    

# 4. Culture & Norms

•       Over-communicate when things are blocked 

•       Ask for help early, not after you've spent 3 hours stuck

•       Disagree openly, commit fully — once a decision is made, we move together

•       We work hard, but we don't celebrate burning out!!
